## How to sign

You do not need to sign a physical document. When you open a pull request, the
**CLA-assistant bot** will automatically comment on your PR. To sign:

1. Read this Agreement in full.
2. Reply on the PR with the exact phrase the bot indicates (typically: *"I have
   read the CLA Document and I hereby sign the CLA"*).
3. Your GitHub identity is recorded as the signer. You only need to sign **once
   per GitHub account** — all your future PRs to Jitre are automatically
   covered.

If you have questions about this CLA before signing, open an issue or contact
the Maintainer via [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/yamil-lazzari/).
